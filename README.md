# Health & Safety Committee — Monthly Meeting Guide

A single-file, click-through slide deck that walks the committee through the
monthly Health & Safety meeting, step by step. Built to match our standard
TELUS-branded deck style (same layout, colours, and navigation as the
`techhub-assist-pitch` deck).

## What's inside

- **Hero** — greeting and overview.
- **Step 1** — Attendance, date & quorum (opens the committee Google Sheet).
- **Step 2** — Log monthly SM attendance in OATS (with one-click copy of the codes).
- **Step 3** — Monthly committee inspections.
- **Step 4** — HOIRs, SROs & open business (where to find each + held-business example).
- **Step 5** — Open floor and reminders.
- **Closer** — five-step recap.

## Using it

Open `index.html` in any browser. Move through the slides with the
**Back / Next** buttons, the **→ / ←** arrow keys, **Space**, or the dots.
Each slide deep-links by hash (`#1`…`#7`), so you can share a link straight
to a step.

## Notes

- Self-contained: one HTML file plus two self-hosted fonts in `fonts/`. No build step, no dependencies.
- This is an internal working document. It carries `noindex` so it won't be picked up by search engines.
